VISION OF THE ORGANISATION

A self-reliant, empowered, and protected refugee community where every vulnerable person lives with dignity, access to basic needs, and equal opportunities.

MISSION

To support and empower vulnerable refugees through community-based initiatives that promote protection, education, health awareness, and improved living conditions.

GOAL

To improve the quality of life of vulnerable individuals in Bidibidi Refugee Settlement through sustainable community support programs.

CORE OBJECTIVES

To improve shelter conditions for older persons and vulnerable women.

To reduce teenage pregnancy through community awareness and education.

To promote girl child education and school retention.

To educate adolescent girls on menstrual hygiene management.

To support orphans and vulnerable children with scholastic materials.

To strengthen youth participation in community development initiatives.

COHESIVE PEOPLE'S ORGANISATION WORKING POLICY

CPO operates under the following principles:

1. Non-Discrimination: Services are provided without discrimination based on nationality, gender, religion, or background.

2. Volunteerism: Activities are driven by committed refugee youth volunteers.

3. Community Participation: Programs are implemented with active involvement of beneficiaries.

4. Accountability & Transparency: Proper documentation and responsible use of resources.

5. Child Protection & Gender Sensitivity: Ensuring safety and dignity of children and women in all activities.

COHESIVE PEOPLE'S ORGANISATION ACTIVITIES

1. Shelter Support. Repairing houses for elderly persons. Repairing houses for vulnerable women and female-headed households.

2.Teenage Pregnancy Awareness Community sensitization meetings. Youth dialogue sessions. School outreach programs.

3. Girl Child Education Promotion Awareness campaigns on the importance of educating girls. Community engagement with parents and guardians.

4. Menstrual Hygiene Education Educating adolescent girls on proper menstrual hygiene practices. Promoting dignity and confidence among young girls.

5. Educational Support Providing scholastic materials to orphans and vulnerable children. Encouraging school enrollment and retention.

TARGET BENEFICIARIES

1. Older persons.

2. Vulnerable women.

3. Adolescent girls.

4. Orphans and vulnerable children.

5. Refugee youth.

PARTNERSHIP APPROACH

CPO works collaboratively with community leaders, schools, parents, youth groups, and humanitarian stakeholders operating in Bidibidi Refugee Settlement.
